About Flummox

Flum • mox (v) /ˈfləməks/ Is an informal english word means to perplex (someone) greatly, confuse someone (so much) that they do not know what to do.

Probably, this word sounds strange to our ears. I just found this word a week ago when I had no idea to name this site. The reason behind my decision to use this word as a title is because it is simple and pretty cool word. In addition, I know that behind the tittle sometimes contains meaning. So that I hope this flummoxing site will give you wider vision about something around you.
